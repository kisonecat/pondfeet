pondfeet

How do I use this?

Use your mouse to draw characters. As you draw, look to your right. Being somewhat optimistic, you will see a full character set respond to your stylistic choices.

How does this work?

I downloaded a large collection of bitmap fonts and then trained a neural network in PyTorch to predict missing glyphs, given the shapes of the glyphs you provide.
